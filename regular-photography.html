What Regular Photography Means

Regular Photography focuses on traditional, posed shots—perfect for events, studio portraits, and formal gatherings. It emphasizes balance, lighting, and consistency across every image.

  • Classic Portrait & Event Coverage: Professional framing and clean edits.
  • Posed & Group Shots: We guide subjects for the best angles and harmony.
  • Consistent Style: Unified color tones and editing for a cohesive album.
  • Studio & On-Location: Choose between a studio setup or a natural backdrop.
  • Deliverables: 30–100 edited high-resolution JPEGs and web-optimized versions.
